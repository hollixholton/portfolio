CHILLY CHILE'S PACKAGING REDESIGN

Chilly Chile's is a spicy food store in Ottawa, ON. Over the winter holidays they offer their customers the chance to buy gift sets for their loved ones. I was asked to take the current design and update it so that it aligns with their brand.

APPLICATION

The store's atmosphere is bright and fun, with a bit of humour mixed in there. This was something I felt was important to have portrayed in the designs since it makes Chilly Chile's unique to its competition.
